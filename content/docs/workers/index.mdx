## What are Workers?

Workers are **automation agents** that run predefined tasks to move, transform, and process data. They handle repetitive work reliably, ensure that transformations happen on schedule, and keep your data pipeline running smoothly without manual intervention.

Think of them as the **engines** that power your data workflows.
